About POPEYE

H6 is the next generation of Mercedes-Benz truck. The environment of truck industry in China is totally different from the other developed countries. In order to deliver the best service for China local truck customers, Lab1886 team and H6 connectivity team co-incubated the 1st MVP version H6 connectivity system.

My Role

I was the lead designer and worked closely with another UI designer & PM, finished all the design including the Website and Android of connectivity system.

Traget Customer

1. Fleet owner
2. Fleet manager
3. Truck driver(in future)

Research

According to the report from the research team, there are 7 modules which were identified as actively used by fleets.

Track & trace:Real time show and record of truck driving route to bring transparency in driving route and cost Driver monitor: Real time driving monitor using camera, ADAS, etc. to increase driving safety Driver grading: Driving behavior analysis to increase driving safety, reduce fuel consumption and increase spare parts life time Vehicle monitor: Real time monitor of cargo container to per-decided abnormal alert Maintenance support:

  • - Real time troubleshooting and fault record
  • - Predictive maintenance to improve vehicle life time and reduce repair cost
Remote shut down: Support financial leasing companies to ensure asset safety Report: Enable and support logistic company business analysis

Ideation

In order to hear the voice from our internal truck experts, we also had an internal ideation workshop in April, 2019. 16 experts from 13 departments are attended. In the workshop, attendees were separated into 3 groups. Each group stands for different customer unit and comes up ideas from different perspectives. Through the internal ideation, we collected 91 frictions, for 8 main frictions, 47 solutions and in the end we got our top 3 ideas.
